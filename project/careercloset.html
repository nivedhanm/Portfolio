Overview

Texas A&M Career Closet is a quasi-student organization that is supported by the foundation for former students and the career center at A&M. Their purpose is simple, to provide a suit rental service to the thousands of students who are searching for jobs the first time in their lives and don't own a suit to wear to the interview. This project documents how we developed a new responsive website for them.

PROBLEM

In their existing website, a student needs to schedule an appointment, visit the office physically during allotted time slot to rent a suit and return it within the due date. Currently, they have a static website that shows basic information about their business and a link to schedule appointments that routes to a third party website.

See Original Website

1. There was a lack of an availability check which was an important user task

2. The External Website did not allow for custom administration

3. The External calendar did not filter out days that were Holidays

There were several other issues such as branding compliance and improved layout etc. The biggest task with this website was that of redesigning the information architecture and managing the taxonomy. The entire structure was reorganized to simplify the flow.

As a part of the project I created the Design Persona and mascot(above). It is simple friendly penguin in a top hat and monocle that greets visitors with messages like "Top of the morning to you ol' chap, could I perhaps interest you in a cup of tea?" We followed Mr. Aaron Walter's principles of 'Designing for Emotion' throughout the project
