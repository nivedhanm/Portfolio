This was a simple responsive web design project for a course that I am currently taking. But instead of creating a random website my goal was to create something that would be real useful. Texas A&M Career Closet is a quasi-student organization that is supported by the foundation for former students and the career center at A&M. Their purpose is simple, to provide a suit rental service to the thousands of students who are searching for jobs the first time in their lives and don't own a suit to wear to the interview. We reached out to The Career Closet and offered to build a website for them.

As a part of the project I created the Design Persona and mascot(above). It is simple friendly penguin in a top hat and monocle that greets visitors with messages like "Top of the morning to you ol' chap, could I perhaps interest you in a cup of tea?"We followed Mr. Aaron Walter's principles of 'Designing for Emotion' throughout the project

The biggest task with this website was that of redesigning the information architecture and managing the taxonomy. The entire structure was reorganized to simplify the flow.
